Marvel Movies

History

The first film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe was Iron Man (2008), which was distributed by Paramount Pictures.
Since 2008, Marvel Studios has released 32 films from Iron Man (2008) to Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3(2023)
